What are the main symptoms ?

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways.
Most infected people will develop mild to moderate
illness and recover without hospitalization.

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath

things to do during covid

It is important to keep in mind that most people that contract
the virus face mild symptoms and recover relatively easily. The
mortality rate of Coronavirus has also been more or less the same
over the past 6 weeks. Hence, when looked at it objectively,
a smooth recovery from the virus can be achieved.
